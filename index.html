How I Work

Research Process

  1. 1

    🔍 Survey the problem

    Read the literature, find the gap, and pin down real hardware constraints from day one.

  2. 2

    📊 Data & baseline

    Build the data pipeline, standardize the benchmark, and reproduce baselines for a trustworthy reference point.

  3. 3

    🧪 Design the architecture

    Propose the model, run ablation studies on every component, and tune based on numbers rather than intuition.

  4. 4

    🗜️ Compress & optimize

    Distillation, FP16 quantization, and ONNX → TensorRT conversion to cut latency without losing accuracy.

  5. 5

    🚀 Deploy & measure

    Ship to the edge device, profile FPS and power draw, then write it up with reproducible results.
